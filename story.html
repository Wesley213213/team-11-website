Welcome to Coltivare

Home Story & History Management Team Products Services Testimonials

Coltivare was founded in 1998 by a small group of passionate agriculturalists in the heart of the Midwest.

With deep roots in sustainable farming and a vision to modernize the agricultural industry, our company has grown from a humble seed to a full-fledged enterprise offering products and services tailored to farmers worldwide.

Over the last two decades, we have embraced innovation, fostered community partnerships, and championed responsible land stewardship.

Our Journey

  1. 1998: Founded in Indiana by five farmers.
  2. 2005: Expanded into seed distribution and soil testing services.
  3. 2015: Launched digital agriculture tools for precision farming.
  4. 2022: Opened our international division serving South America.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation in agriculture
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Supporting local farmers and communities
