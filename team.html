James Smith

Founder & CEO

James grew up on his family’s farm in Iowa and has a deep-rooted passion for agriculture. After earning an agricultural-engineering degree from Purdue University, he founded Coltivare to innovate farming practices. His vision has transformed Coltivare into a leader in sustainable agriculture.

"Agriculture is the backbone of our world, and innovation is its future."
